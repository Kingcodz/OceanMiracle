Our Story

Ocean Miracle LTD was founded in 2010 by Kingsley Chinonye Unije, a veteran in the building materials industry with over 20 years of experience. What began as a small operation has grown into a leading supplier of premium building materials and electrical components across the region.

In our early years, we focused primarily on electrical components, establishing strong relationships with manufacturers known for their quality and reliability. As our reputation for exceptional products and customer service grew, so did our product range and customer base.

By 2015, we had expanded our operations to include a comprehensive range of building materials, catering to both residential and commercial construction projects. Today, Ocean Miracle LTD serves thousands of customers, from individual homeowners to large construction firms, maintaining our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Cookie Policy Overview

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. We use the following types of cookies:
